This plugin adds links to your social networking sites' profiles in a box floating at the left side of the screen.

== Description ==

= What is Social Slider? =

The Social Slider plugin display on your blog an expandable box, that has links to your profiles in different social sites.

By default, the box is attached to the left side of the browser window, but you can easily put it on the right side.

= Configuration options =

Social Slider plugin can be easily customized. You can set such options as:

* **Display mode** - you can choose between several display modes: big icon set, small icon set, icons without captions, icons with a place for an ad or a Facebook widget.
* **Position of the box** - the blog's owner can set on what height the box will be displayed
* **Color scheme** - choose between two color schemes (light and dark) to match Social Slider with your blog's template  (theme)
* **Transparency** - an option of setting the transparency of the Social Slider box has been added for the demanding users
* **Speed of expanding** - after hovering, Social Slider can expand smoothly or show up instantly. It's up to you :)
* **Mobile browsers** - you can disable Social Slider for mobile browsers (iPhone, Android...) in order to keep the page rendered correctly on mobile devices

== Installation ==

After you activate the plugin, go to the configuration page and enter the URLs of your profiles on social networking sites. If you don't use a particular site, leave the field blank - the icon will not be displayed.
