----------- Overview -----------

Super Graphics Bros. was designed to be a campus-themed 3D side-scroller
based on the Mario franchise, with a twist on typical side-scroller camera motion.

It stars two lovable characters you can switch between at any time:
The University of Maryland computer graphics professors!
(David Mount and Ahmitabh Varshney)
